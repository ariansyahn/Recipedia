Apple or Rhubarb Crumble
Ingredients
››3 cooking apples or 6 stalks of rhubarb
››2oz or 50g sugar
››3 dessertspoons water
Topping
››6oz or 180g flour
››2oz or 50g caster sugar
››2oz or 50g margarine
››2 heaped dessertspoons of porridge oats
Method
1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C or 350°F or Gas Mark 4.
2. Put sliced apples (rhubarb), sugar and water in a saucepan and cook
gently until they are soft. Place cooked fruit mixture in a pie dish.
3. Sieve flour into a bowl. Rub in margarine. Add sugar and mix
thoroughly.
4. Sprinkle crumble mixture over fruit mixture.
5. Bake in the pre-heated oven for 30 minutes.
