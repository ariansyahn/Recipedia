Banana Bread
Ingredients
››0oz or 280g self-raising flour
››5oz or 140g brown sugar
››3 eggs, beaten
››3 bananas, mashed
››½ teaspoon baking powder
››4oz or 110g walnuts chopped
››½ teaspoon cinnamon
››½ teaspoon nutmeg
››7oz or 200g melted margarine
Method
1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C or 350°F or Gas Mark 4.
2. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl.
3. Mix the wet ingredients in another bowl.
4. Mix the wet mixture and the dry mixture together.
5. Put into well greased loaf tin.
6. Bake in the pre-heated oven for 1½ hours.
