Beaf Loaf

Ingredients
››1lb or 450g lean minced beef
››1 large onion, very finely chopped
››4oz or 110g fresh brown breadcrumbs (4 slices)
››pinch of salt if desired
››pepper
››2 dessertspoons tomato sauce
››1pt or 575ml of homemade chicken or vegetable
stock (page 22) or 1 low-salt stock cube
dissolved in 1pt or 575ml of boiling water
››1 egg, beaten
Method
1. Place all the ingredients in a large mixing bowl and mix thoroughly together.
2. Brush a large loaf tin with oil and fill with the mixture.
3. Smooth the top with a palette knife and place in the fridge for 1 hour.
4. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C or 350°F or Gas Mark 4.
5. Cook in the pre-heated oven for 1¼–1½ hours.
