Queen Cakes
Ingredients
››4oz or 115g soft margarine
››2 eggs
››8oz or 225g self-raising flour
››4oz or 115g caster sugar
››a little semi-skimmed milk
Method
1. Pre-heat the oven to 200°C or 400°F or Gas Mark 6.
2. Sieve flour into bowl.
3. Cream the margarine and sugar together.
4. Beat the eggs.
5. Add the flour and eggs gradually to the creamed
margarine and sugar, beating well to avoid curdling.
6. If the mixture is dry add a little milk.
7. Divide the mixture into bun cases.
8. Bake in the pre-heated oven for 20 minutes.
