Steamed Egg Chicken
Ingredients:
•	1/4 chicken breast (finely chopped) 
•	1 egg 
•	enough salt 
•	enough pepper 
•	1/2 tsp mushroom broth 
•	2 garlic cloves

Steps:
•	Mix the eggs, garlic and minced chicken into one. 
•	Do not forget to pour salt, mushroom and pepper broth to taste 
•	Steam for about 30 minutes in the dish so that the chicken meat is evenly distributed.
•	Later you can try stabbing with a toothpick to make sure the chicken meat is cooked 
•	ready to be served
