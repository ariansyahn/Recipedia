Tea Scones
Ingredients
››1lb or 450g self-raising flour
››2oz or 50g sugar
››4oz or 110g margarine
››1 egg
››water or semi-skimmed milk
Method
1. Pre-heat the oven to 200°C or 375°F or Gas Mark 6.
2. Put flour and sugar into a mixing bowl. Rub in the margarine.
3. Add the beaten eggs with sufficient water or milk to make a nice
soft dough.
4. Put on a floured surface and roll to ½ inch thickness. Cut
with a knife or pastry cutter, brush over with egg wash
or a little milk and put on a floured baking tray.
5. Bake in the pre-heated oven for 15–20 minutes.
