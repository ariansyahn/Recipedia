Baked Potato with Turkey and Ham Topping
Ingredients
››2 large baked potatoes
››1 dessertspoon of peas
››1 teaspoon of low-fat mayonnaise
››4oz or 110g cooked turkey
››4oz or 110g cooked ham
Method:
Potato
1. Pre-heat the oven to 220°C or
425°F or Gas Mark 7.
2. Wash the potatoes, then prick
them all over with a fork
3. Bake in a pre-heated oven for 1½
hours or until the inside is tender.
4. For the microwave method, follow the
instructions given in the manual.
Filling
1. Cut the potato in half and carefully scoop the
centre out of the potato.
2. Slice the turkey and ham and add to the
mashed potato flesh.
3. Stir in peas and a little mayonnaise.
4. Add a little salt and pepper.
5. Return the mixture to the potato skins.
6. Place in a hot oven and bake until warmed
through and slightly browned on top.
