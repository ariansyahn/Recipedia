Kemangi Sauce Chicken
Ingredients:
•	1/2 kg of cured chicken 
•	1 bunch of basil 
•	2 carrots (shaved) 
•	5 red chilies 
•	30 cayenne fruit 
•	6 red onion cloves 
•	4 garlic cloves 
•	2 tomatoes 
•	1 cm ginger 
•	1 cm kencur 
•	Galangal 
•	3 orange leaves 
•	2 bay leaves
•	Sugar 
•	Salt 
•	Pepper 
•	Taste flavor

Steps:
•	The chicken that has been eaten fried is then brewed. 
•	Blend the shallots, garlic, tomatoes, ginger, kencur, red chili, and cayenne pepper. 
•	Saute with orange leaves, bay leaves, and galangal until fragrant. Add water. 
•	Add chicken and carrots. Then follow with the basil leaves included.
•	Cook until the spices are evenly spread. 
•	Ready to serve.


Teriyaki Chicken 
Ingredients:
•	500 gr Long sliced chicken meat 
•	1 pcs Onions, lengthwise slices 
•	1/2 tsp Sugar 
•	1/4 tsp Salt
•	150 ml Water 
•	Oil for sautéing
Soaking seasoning: 
•	3 tbsp Teriyaki sauce 
•	2 tbsp Oyster sauce 
•	1 tbsp Sweet soy sauce 
•	1 tsp Sesame oil 
•	3 cloves of garlic, puree 
•	2 cm Ginger, puree 
•	1/4 tsp Pepper
•	1/2 tsp Powder broth

Steps:
•	Mix the chicken with marinade, sugar and salt, leave the mixture 1-2 hours so that the spices permeate.
•	Heat the oil saute the meat until done, pour the water, add the onion, cook until the water shrinks slightly, thicken with the cornstarch

Chicken Bones Noodle
Ingredients:
•	1/4 kg chicken bone 
•	1 small pack of pigeon noodles 
•	5 garlic cloves 
•	3 red onion cloves 
•	7 cayenne pepper 
•	1 spring onion 
•	2 celery stalks 
•	1 1/2 tbsp pepper powder 
•	2 tablespoons chili sauce 
•	3 tablespoons sweet soy sauce 
•	to taste Sugar and salt
•	Oil to saute

Steps:
•	Wash thoroughly then boil chicken bones for 10 minutes. Then drain 
•	Boil the noodles until half done. Then drain 
•	Blend onion, garlic and cayenne pepper 
•	Saute until fragrant spices with sliced celery and leeks. Then add pepper powder, sugar and salt. 
•	Enter the chicken bones, wait until the spices are absorbed. 
•	Add noodles, sauce and soy sauce. Then saute until the spices are mixed.
•	Add sugar and salt if needed.
