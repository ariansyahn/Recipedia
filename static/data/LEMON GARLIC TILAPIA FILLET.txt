LEMON GARLIC TILAPIA FILLET
INGREDIENTS
FOR 4 PEOPLE
• 4 fish fillets
• 2 garlic cloves
• 1 bunch of coriander
• 3 spoons of lemon juice
• 1 spoon of sunflower oil or butter
• salt and pepper

Steps:
1. Rinse fish fillets in clean water, pat dry 
2. Place fillets in shallow cooking pan
3. Pour lemon juice and oil over the fillets,
sprinkle with garlic, coriander, salt and
pepper
Note: do not add any water
4. Cover with a tight lid and cook with low
heat for 5 minutes
5. Serve immediately with banana stew,
mash potatoes, cornmeal, cassava or rice
