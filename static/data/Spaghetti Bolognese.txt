Spaghetti Bolognese
Ingredients
››½ lb or 225g lean mince
beef or lamb
››½ pt or 275ml of homemade
beef stock (page 22) or ½ low
salt beef stock cube dissolved in
½ pt or 275ml of boiling water
››½ onion finely chopped
››1 dessertspoon tomato sauce
››pinch of salt if desired
››pepper
››1 dessertspoon flour
››1 tin of tomatoes
››2 mushrooms peeled
and chopped
››½ red pepper chopped
››½ carrot chopped
››8 oz spaghetti
Method
1. Cook the mince beef or lamb, onions and mushrooms on a dry
pan (low heat) for 15 minutes. (Sufficient fat in mince for frying).
2. Add flour and stir well.
3. Add the stock and before it comes to the boil add
the vegetables, tin of tomatoes, tomato sauce,
salt and pepper. Cook gently for 45 minutes.
4. Cook the spaghetti for 10–15 minutes in boiling salted water. Strain.
5. Serve with the bolognese sauce on top.
