Wholemeal Bread
Ingredients
››1lb or 425g wholemeal flour
››1 dessertspoon wheatgerm
››1 dessertspoon bran
››½ pt or 275ml butter milk
››1 teaspoon breadsoda
››1 teaspoon brown sugar
››1 teaspoon salt
››2 teaspoons sunflower oil
››1 egg (optional)
Method
1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C or 350°F or Gas Mark 4.
2. Mix all the ingredients well together.
Then pour the lot into a lightly oiled loaf tin.
3. Bake in the pre-heated oven for 15 minutes.
4. Reduce heat to 150°C or 300°F or Gas Mark 2 and
bake for a further 40 minutes.
